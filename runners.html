The miles, the time commitment, the constant pounding of foot against pavement are a challenge no doubt, but for us on Team World Vision that's only half the battle. We run to bring clean water to children and communities that desperately need it, but those types of things take money. Asking friends and family to fork over their money isn't a conversation most people are rushing to have, but it's the reason we do this thing.
